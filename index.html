What we do

ABOUT US

TUIT Students’ Union, or shortly SU, is students’ self-governance body. By students, for students, it serves to assist, support and engage students in a range of exciting and valuable experiences, protect students’ rights and represent all TUIT students in all communications with internal and external parties.

Our Goals

Everything I expect, and more

“To gather all like-minded students and engage them all in as many activities as possible”

“To help students win academic & research exchange opportunities abroad”

“To provide students with field-specialized short-term excursions and internships at local companies”

“To share ideas, discuss issues, and provide solutions to common problems at university”

“To popularize the prestige of the university”

“To build a competitive environment for growth among students”

REGULATIONS

Regulations is the important component of TUIT SU. We have written our own constitution which states each executive’s liabilities and their permissions to operate within community. It also includes the procedure of election system and other vital rules.

Download SU Constitution to learn more

Our Brand

  • The gold color stands for the prestige of the community;
  • The font of TUIT SU is unique from the others, showing more modern technical font;
  • The crown means a lot:
    • its parabolic shape which also resembles “U” means Union;
    • the such crown itself was considered one of the biggest legacies during Caesar's time meaning triumph and independence;
    • the wheat leaves of the crown mean we are not alone - there are a lot of us and we stand together.
Meet The Team
SU President

Shokhrukh Yursunjonov

Cybersecurity

President is the first person who represents interests of students of Tashkent University of Information Technologies to the Administration and all other viable institutions interested.

SU Vise President

Kamronbek Soliboev

Digital Economics

General Manager or Vice President represents "judicial power", controls the legality of the projects and activities of other SU members and other students’ associations at all. He/She is also considered as an ambassador to build partnerships with other organizations with the approval of the President outside the university.

SU General Secretary

Jamshidbek Yusupov

Digital Economics

General Secretary manages the SU fund. General Secretary can invest to projects approved by the SU Board. He is responsible for organizing fund- raising projects within the University and General Secretary has the right to initiate fund-raising projects outside the University too.

SU Education and Welfare Executive

Alisher Akhmadov

Cybersecurity

Education and Welfare Executive is responsible for the academic support of the SU Board to the students of Tashkent University of Information Technologies. Education and Welfare Executive is the Representative in Chief for the Academic and Welfare Matters within the University and Dormitory. He or she is responsible for any educational activity clubs such as, for example, Zakovat, STEM Clubs, Language Clubs, and more.

SU Events and Entertainment Executive

Oysarakhon Makhmudova

Digital Economics

Events and Entertainment Executive is responsible for the Social Activities and Events in the University and Complex Projects’ social activities. He or she is responsible for Social, Art, Entertaining, Music, Drama Clubs of TUIT, and more.

SU Information and Communication Executive

Mukhiddin Alisherov

Cybersecurity

Information and Communication Executive is responsible for providing and maintaining the appropriate level and channel of communication between Students and the SU Board members via e-mails, available media and other forms of electronic and printed communication. He or she is responsible for Social Media pages management, Web-Site content management, Public Announcement and Polls on pages, and more.

SU Sport Events Executive

Iftikhor Jurakulov

Cybersecurity

SU Sport Events Executive is responsible for the all Sport Events at TUIT and for all Sport Clubs at TUIT. Also has a privilege right to organize Sport purpose Events. In case where the same project was planned earlier and written by other SU Executives, student(s) or students’ association(s), then SU Sport Events Executive should be stated as Senior Partner on the same project and should be aware of the progress of the project.
